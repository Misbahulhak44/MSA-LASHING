About Us

We prevent containers from falling into sea.

Nature of Business

We are the pioneer and the only manufacturers of Container Lashing Equipment in India under the brand name “ MSA ”. We are located at the port of Mumbai ( India ), and conveniently accessible from fast developing and strategically located container port of JNPT( India ).

Facilities and Capabilities

We did not just get into the container business, we in fact founded it nearly three decades ago. We started as a small company with two items of lashing equipment, but now manufacturing many vital and important container lashing equipment.

Mission Statement

Since,foundation of our company we have always focused on quality and reliability of the product, taking into consideration it’s designing and conveniency of its operation. All our lashing equipment is user’s - friendly where we ensured its simplicity, smoothness and trouble free performance with a minimum and economical maintenance.

We are specialised in:

Our products include “ MSA ” Semi-automatic twistlock, hanging stackers, mid locks, manual twistlock, Bridge-Fittings, Single and Double-Stackers, Lashing Rod and Truck Twistlock, etc.
